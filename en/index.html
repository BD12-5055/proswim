01 · About

From the blocks of Rome to the open water of Flanders.

Born in Ghent in 1991, I came up through MEGA under coach Herman Verbauwen and built my career around the 200 m and 400 m freestyle. I made my senior debut at the 2009 World Championships in Rome and reached a 4×200 freestyle relay final at the 2010 Europeans in Budapest before stepping away from competition in 2012.

Today I coach the people who used to look at the swim leg with dread. Age-groupers, first-timers, sub-elite triathletes — I bring twenty-plus years of pool craft to a discipline most triathletes never properly learn. Honest feedback, clear structure, no fluff.
